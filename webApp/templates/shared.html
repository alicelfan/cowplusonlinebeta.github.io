How to Upload Datasets for Public Use

  1. The upload function only accepts “.csv” files. Please make sure that your datasets have the following variables. If you do not have these variables, an alert will show up and your file will not pass verification for uploading.
    1. For monadic datasets: stateabb (state abbreviations), ccode (country codes), year
    2. For dyadic datasets: stateabb1, ccode1, stateabb2, ccode2, year
    3. For country codes and state abbreviations, please refer to the Correlates of War website.
  2. Click the “Verify” button. It may take some time for your data to pass verification, especially if you have a lot of variables.
  3. After your file has passed verification, simply click “Upload.” Once again, it may take some time to upload if you have large datasets (>50 MB). Both buttons will be grayed out, indicating that the upload process has begun.
  4. To delete your datasets from the Shared page, simply click “Delete.” To download datasets to your computer, click “Download.” To import datasets to your uploaded datasets list, click “Import.” Finally, to preview datasets before downloading or importing datasets, click “Preview.”

Note: Files uploaded here will be publicly accessible and available for anyone to use and download. If you wish to upload files for personal use, please visit the Upload page.
